Activa
Rebrand
Elevate Activia from a functional product to a vibrant, sustainable choice in a balanced lifestyle.
Role: Lead Designer
2024
Packaging Design
Commercial Ad
Activia evolves from a clinical gut-health brand into a vibrant, sustainable lifestyle choice that energizes health-conscious, sustainable consumers.
The aim of the rebrand is to move away from sterile packaging and adopt a vibrant visual identity with warm colors like coral, mint, and terracotta. This approach will create an inviting feel, appealing to younger demographics who appreciate joyful and lively products while being mindful of their environmental impact. It will elevate Activia from a functional product to a vibrant, sustainable choice in a balanced lifestyle. By adopting a warm design, emphasizing feel-good messaging, and showcasing commitment to sustainability, Activia will connect with 2024 consumers who seek enjoyment and peace of mind while prioritizing eco-friendly living.
